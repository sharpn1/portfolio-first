About Me

Welcome to my portfolio, a vibrant tapestry of creativity and innovation. My passion for design and dedication to academic excellence converge in my work. A background in Fashion and Business has honed my ability to think expansively, blending artistic flair with a strong academic foundation. I've channeled my creativity and knowledge into crafting a diverse range of visual assets, including logos, book covers, posters, leaflets, booklets, and artwork. Beyond design, my interests span adventurous treks, baking, painting, and gardening. As a devoted food enthusiast, I indulge in exploring culinary creations while consistently seeking new avenues to express my creativity and expand my expertise. Presently, I'm fully immersed in a Front-end Web Development bootcamp, driven by ambition, confidence, and an unwavering commitment to my career aspirations. Drawing from a rich tapestry of experiences, I approach every challenge with the belief that I possess the capability to excel in any endeavor I pursue.
